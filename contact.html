SignLens

Our mission is to develop innovative and accessible communication solutions that empower the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. We aim to eliminate communication barriers by leveraging advanced technology, such as smart glasses that translate American Sign Language (ASL) into text and speech in real-time. Our vision is to create a world where individuals with hearing impairments can communicate effortlessly in all aspects of life, from education to social interactions and beyond. We believe in inclusivity, innovation, and accessibility as core values, ensuring that our solutions are user-centered, technologically advanced, and make a tangible difference in people's lives. Through our work, we strive to foster a society where everyone, regardless of hearing ability, can fully participate and engage in their communities.
