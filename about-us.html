Who Are We

Our organization, founded with the goal of improving communication accessibility, is focused on creating innovative solutions for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. The organization began as a small team of passionate individuals with diverse backgrounds in technology, healthcare, and social advocacy. We recognized the significant barriers faced by the deaf community in everyday communication, especially in environments such as schools, workplaces, and public spaces. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge assistive technology that bridges these communication gaps, starting with our current projectâ€”smart glasses that translate American Sign Language (ASL) into text and speech in real time. Key accomplishments so far include successful prototype development, early-stage user testing, and establishing partnerships with educational and healthcare organizations. We are committed to improving the lives of individuals with hearing impairments by providing them with the tools they need to communicate seamlessly and engage fully in society.

Our Services

Our mission is to develop innovative and accessible communication solutions that empower the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. We aim to eliminate communication barriers by leveraging advanced technology, such as smart glasses that translate American Sign Language (ASL) into text and speech in real-time. Our vision is to create a world where individuals with hearing impairments can communicate effortlessly in all aspects of life, from education to social interactions and beyond. We believe in inclusivity, innovation, and accessibility as core values, ensuring that our solutions are user-centered, technologically advanced, and make a tangible difference in people's lives. Through our work, we strive to foster a society where everyone, regardless of hearing ability, can fully participate and engage in their communities.

SignLens Community Connect

A volunteer-driven platform that pairs skilled sign language translators and advocates with individuals and organizations to facilitate real-time communication support, fostering inclusivity in spontaneous and critical scenarios.
